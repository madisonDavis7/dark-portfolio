Scoop and Joy

Colorful and Inviting
Good Use of Iconology
Fluid and Dynamic Design

This design was a really fun one to do. I usually work with more muted and neutral colors so designing a website for a fake ice cream shop was a needed change. I wanted this site to be very bright, have colorful gradients, and reflect a summer feeling. I played around with different shapes to break up the information and give it that playful vibe.

The main inspiration for this site came from a color palette I found on Pinterest. The colors really popped and inspired me to design something bright and fun, especially because this was made in the middle of winter. This design let me explore more natural shapes and a less rigid structure, it was out of my comfort zone but taught me to be more flexible and broaden my design skillset.

Learned:

  1. FFigma Shape PLugins
  2. Using Gradients for Colorful Effects
  3. Logo Design
  4. Creating a Mobile Design
