Responsive Web Design

This was the first time I used Figma and the first website I designed on my own. The goal was to go through the entire process, from drawing a light sketch on paper, creating a mood board of ideas, and finalizing the prototype in Figma. Because I wanted to focus on the UI I didn't add any interactability or animations, I kept it simple to learn the basics.

I began by creating a fake company and a fake branding. I decided to go with a solar power company because it went well with the color palette I had in mind. I wanted the colors to be calming but modern, to feel sophisticated and represent a new startup compnay. This was a fun project that let me famaliarize myself with Figma, RWD, color theory, and page layouts.
